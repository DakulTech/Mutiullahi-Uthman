The Human Behind the Code

Would you like to know
more about me?
Come with me →

I hold a degree in Agricultural Economics and Extension from the prestigious Ladoke Akintola University of Technology (LAUTECH), graduating in 2016. You might be wondering — how does an agricultural graduate end up in Software Engineering?

It happened as a challenge. After graduating, I ventured into cryptocurrency trading and kept at it for years. Then, around 2020 — out of pandemic boredom and a genuine spark of ambition — I wanted to launch my own crypto token. I'd already drafted the whitepaper and mapped everything out.

The software agency I contacted quoted a price I simply couldn't afford. That one moment killed the idea — and lit a fire in me that hasn't gone out since.

From that day I made myself a promise: I was going to learn to write code, build my own projects, and use software engineering to solve real problems in the world. And here I am today — living exactly that promise.
